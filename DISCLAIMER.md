# Disclaimer

SETHX Client is open-source software for interacting with the SETHX protocol through a user-controlled wallet.

Users are responsible for reviewing all transaction details in their wallet before signing. Smart contract interaction involves technical, market, liquidity, oracle, network, and wallet risks.

The client does not hold user assets or private keys. Transactions are signed through the user's wallet and submitted to the connected network.

Users are responsible for determining whether use of the software and protocol is permitted in their jurisdiction.
