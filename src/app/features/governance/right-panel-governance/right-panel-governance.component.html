Governance Actions
Before signing

Review proposal state, target contracts, calldata, vote counts, and the connected wallet's voting power. Governance actions can change protocol settings, markets, roles, or treasury state.

Voting flow
1. Select an account and confirm SETHX ownership
2. Delegate voting power
3. Draft, load, or review a proposal
4. Cast Against, For, or Abstain
